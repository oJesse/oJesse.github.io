Seven Scrolls

A compact roguelike with a modular spell system. Endless possibilities, intricate chain reactions and ever different play sessions. Out now!

Play Japanese

Play Japanese is a growing collection of games and apps to help with studying the Japanese language. Although there is a lot of educational software for beginners, there is less for intermediate and advanced learners. I try to fill that gap and learn some myself at the same time.

Eat Me Please (They Need To Be Fed 1, 2 & 3)

A platformer game with 360 degrees of gravity. Featuring 100s of levels!

Heads Off (私、転がります。)

A game I designed at my current employer. Heads are rolling, and they scream when you touch the screen. Control them with the force of their screams, collect souls and unlock new crazy heads. "Aaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaahhh!!!". 1.5+ million downloads.
